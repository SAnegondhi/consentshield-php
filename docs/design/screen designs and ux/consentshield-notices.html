Outreach

The campaign tracks but does not send messages. Export the affected list as CSV and load it into your messaging system (Resend, Mailchimp, Notion + outreach team, etc.). Once recipients click your re-consent link and submit a new consent event referencing v4, the chain auto-marks them resolved.

Sprint-2.4 deferral: bulk re-consent reminder send + per-channel A/B is out of scope for v1. Operators export CSVs and send via their own messaging stack.
