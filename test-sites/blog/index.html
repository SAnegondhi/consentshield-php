On the subtle art of not over-tracking

For most content sites, anonymised page-view analytics is enough to know what is working. Marketing pixels, retargeting, and session replay tools add weight, legal exposure, and audit surface without proportional value.

This page loads a single analytics tracker — Google Analytics — only after you accept. Nothing else. The consent banner is a small corner prompt, not a full-screen takeover.

When you reject, no third-party requests fire from this page. You can verify this in the browser network panel.
